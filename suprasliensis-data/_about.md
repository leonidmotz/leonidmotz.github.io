# About the corpus data

This corpus search engine is a by-product of the data analysis work needed for my MA thesis project on *"Studies in Old Bulgarian Nominal Morphology: Stem Class Analogy in Codex Suprasliensis"* (Supervisors: Prof. Roman N. Krivko, Dr. Ivan P. Petrov) at the University of Vienna's Institute of Slavonic Studies.

The corpus uses the annotated XML from the TOROT/PROIEL project to which I added etymological stem class and declension type information.

To find out more about the TOROT project, visit their [website](https://torottreebank.github.io/). Please also see their [GitHub](https://github.com/syntacticus/syntacticus-treebank-data/tree/main/torot) and the journal article:

> Hanne Martine Eckhoff and Aleksandrs Berdicevskis. 2015. 'Linguistics vs. digital editions: The Tromsø Old Russian and OCS Treebank'. *Scripta & e-Scripta* 14–15, pp. 9–25.

The corpus data is published under a [CC BY 4.0 license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/deed.en), you can find the annotated data on my [GitHub](https://github.com/leonidmotz/leonidmotz.github.io/tree/main/suprasliensis-data).
